AI-assisted visual stool screening for pet owners

Important safety notice PoopSense is an informational AI tool, not a veterinarian. It cannot diagnose illness, confirm parasites, replace fecal testing or an examination, or provide treatment.
  • Contact a veterinarian promptly when your pet is unwell or the stool is repeatedly abnormal.
  • Seek urgent veterinary care for collapse, weakness, repeated vomiting, breathing trouble, pale gums, severe pain, a swollen abdomen, substantial blood, or black/tarry stool.
  • For possible poisoning, contact a veterinarian or ASPCA Animal Poison Control at (888) 426-4435. Fees may apply.
